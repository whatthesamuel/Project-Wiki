1. How to play
   * Controls
     * W, S, A, D to move
     * Space to jump
     * Move mouse to look around
     * Left click to shoot
     * Right click to aim(zoom in)
     * Esc to open menu, etc...

2. How to exit
   * proper exit menu will be implemented later

   * type ``` exit ``` and press enter
